Here you need to put your own credentials to upload to google drive go to 
https://console.cloud.google.com/ 
Create a project
Make it External (Important!)
press on APIs & Services 
Credentials
Create your own credentials
After that go to OAuth consent screen 
Click Audience 
Make sure the publishing status is on testing
Add at the buttom of the page your own Email

You are good to go!!

After all of that take the credentials file that you downloaded from Google rename it to credentials.json and put it in the same folder as backup.py!
Good luck!
